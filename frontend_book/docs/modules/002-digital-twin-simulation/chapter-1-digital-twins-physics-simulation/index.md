# Chapter 1: Digital Twins & Physics Simulation with Gazebo

This chapter introduces the concept of digital twins in robotics and explains how Gazebo enables realistic physics simulation for humanoid robots. You'll learn about fundamental physics concepts and how they affect robot behavior in simulation environments.

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Define what a digital twin is in the context of robotics
- Explain why simulation is critical before real-world deployment
- Understand the role of Gazebo in the ROS 2 ecosystem
- Describe fundamental physics concepts: gravity, mass, inertia, and collisions
- Understand how physics affects humanoid movement and balance
- Observe physics effects on robot joints and movements in simulation
