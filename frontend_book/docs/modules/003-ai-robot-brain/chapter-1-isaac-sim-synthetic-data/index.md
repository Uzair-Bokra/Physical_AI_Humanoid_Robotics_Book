# Chapter 1: NVIDIA Isaac Sim – Photorealistic Simulation & Synthetic Data

This chapter introduces NVIDIA Isaac Sim and explains how it enables high-fidelity simulation and synthetic data generation for training AI systems in humanoid robotics.

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

- Understand the role of photorealistic simulation in Physical AI
- Explain the capabilities of NVIDIA Isaac Sim for synthetic data generation
- Describe RGB images, depth data, and segmentation masks generation
- Understand domain randomization and generalization techniques
- Explain how Isaac Sim integrates with ROS 2 workflows
- Understand the conceptual pipeline from simulation to data to model training
- Recognize sim-to-real transfer challenges

## Chapter Structure

This chapter is organized into the following sections:
